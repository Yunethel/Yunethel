Our Very Very Great And Mighty Founder:

Owner Info

Dexter Lee, founder of Dextry Knows House(DKH), is an inspring young man who has an extreme passion for pastry making and sharing. He hoped to share his recipe with people all around the world so they could taste how delicious a pastry can be even after the recipe has gone through some changes. Growing up, Dexter has always wanted to become a pastry maker, a baker; now, he has achieved multiple noble prizes for best pastry maker.

Lists of his achievements are: Noble Prize: Pro baker 2008, Noble Prize: Best Global Pastry Maker 2013, Gold Award for best culinary MEN 2013, Silver Award EUROPE pastry competition 2014 and more. Being born in a wealthy family, Dexter knew that he had many privilages and took advantage of it to achieve his dream of becoming a top culinary student, a pastry maker, a wonderful baker.

By the age of 29, he completed his goal, he made history as the baker who has the most culinary awards (50 Awards). He appeared in the GUINNES WORLD RECORD(2020) for his groundbreaking achievement. As he builds his team around the world, he developed this website to share recipes with people who are interested in baking (like YOU).
